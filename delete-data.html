Data Deletion Instructions

If you have authorized Kanun Dedektifi through TikTok and wish to delete your associated data, you may request deletion at any time.

How to Request Data Deletion

  1. Send an email to altaykorkmaz35@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line "Data Deletion Request".
  3. Include your TikTok username or OpenID (if available).

Processing Time

All deletion requests are processed within 30 days.

Deleted Information

  • TikTok authorization information
  • Stored account identifiers
  • Publishing history associated with your account

After deletion is completed, your information will no longer be retained unless required by law.
