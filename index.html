About

Kanun Dedektifi is an automated legal education platform that creates and publishes educational short-form videos explaining Turkish laws, legal rights, court procedures, labor law, tenant rights, consumer protection, criminal law, and other legal topics. The platform is designed for educational purposes only and does not provide legal advice.

How It Works

  • AI generates legal education scripts.
  • Text-to-Speech converts scripts into narration.
  • FFmpeg automatically assembles vertical videos.
  • Videos are reviewed by the owner.
  • Content is published automatically to TikTok and YouTube using official APIs.

Technology

  • OpenAI
  • n8n Automation
  • FFmpeg
  • TikTok Content Posting API
  • YouTube Data API
